About Me

Hi, my name is Michael Julian Alvarez. I am of Puerto Rican decent and a Graphic Design Student who is currently on the verge of graduating from Nassau Community College. I will be attending Farmingdale State College in the Spring Semester of 2024. My secret to design is emotion. If I have the opportunity to put my emotions in my designs, I will shoot for the stars. I also like to show representation and take inspiration of my friends and family in the designs that are showcased on this site. So sit back, and I hope that you enjoy my portfolio.
